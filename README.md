Bike Buyers Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

This project analyzes customer demographics and purchasing behavior to understand which factors influence bike purchases. The dataset contains 1,000 records of potential customers including demographic and socioeconomic attributes.
The analysis is presented through an interactive Power BI dashboard highlighting key insights such as income, age, region, education level, and commute distance.

📊 Dashboard Preview

🎯 Project Objective
The goal of this project is to identify patterns and relationships between customer characteristics and bike purchasing behavior.

Key questions explored:

Which age group is more likely to buy a bike?
Does income level affect bike purchases?
How does commute distance influence the likelihood of buying a bike?
Are there regional differences in purchasing behavior?
What role does education level play in purchasing decisions?

🔍 Key Insights

Customers aged 30–40 show the highest bike purchase rate.
Individuals with medium income levels purchase the most bikes.
Customers with a Bachelor’s degree represent the largest group of bike buyers.
Short commute distances (0–1 miles) are associated with higher bike purchases.
North America has the highest number of bike buyers.
